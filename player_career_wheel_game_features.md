# Player Career Wheel Game

## Features Overview
This document outlines the features of a comprehensive player career wheel game divided into various categories:

### 1. Player Stats
- **Overall Rating**: Measures the player's ability.
- **Stamina**: Determines how long a player can perform under pressure.
- **Skills**: Categories such as speed, strength, agility, and intelligence.
- **Experience Points (XP)**: Gained through gameplay and training.

### 2. Spinning Wheel Mechanics
- **Wheel Design**: A visually appealing 3D spinning wheel.
- **Spin Results**: Random outcomes affecting player progression.
- **Wheel Segments**: Each segment represents different events, training options, or bonuses.

### 3. Skill Progression
- **Leveling System**: Players gain levels through achievements.
- **Skill Trees**: Different paths for specialization (e.g., offense, defense).
- **Skill Points**: Allocated to improve specific abilities.

### 4. Economy System
- **Currency**: In-game currency earned and spent on training, equipment, etc.
- **Transfers**: Buying and selling players in-game.
- **Contracts**: Managing player contracts with different teams.

### 5. Injuries
- **Injury System**: Random injuries that affect player stats temporarily.
- **Recovery Time**: Varies by the severity of the injury.
- **Injury Management**: Players can choose to rest or continue playing.

### 6. Sponsorships
- **Sponsorship Deals**: Players can attract sponsors as their career progresses.
- **Benefits**: Financial bonuses and unique items from sponsors.
- **Negotiation System**: Players can negotiate terms for sponsorship deals.

### 7. Training
- **Training Regimens**: Different types of training available for stats improvement.
- **Motivation Levels**: Affect the effectiveness of training sessions.
- **Trainer Influence**: Quality of trainers can impact skill growth.

### 8. Matches
- **Game Format**: Various match types (e.g., friendly, tournament).
- **Match Outcomes**: Impact on player stats and XP.
- **Team Dynamics**: Relationships with teammates affecting performance.

### 9. Achievements
- **Milestones**: Specific goals set for players to achieve.
- **Reward System**: Earn badges, unique items, or in-game currency for achievements.
- **Leaderboard**: Track accomplishments against others in the game.

### 10. User Interface
- **Dashboard**: Central hub displaying player stats, achievements, and current progress.
- **Notifications**: Alerts for spin results, injuries, and training updates.
- **Customization Options**: Players can customize their avatars and teams.

### Implementation Plan
1. **Project Setup**: Initialize project structure for the game.
2. **Core Mechanics Development**: Implement player stats, spinning wheel logic, and skill progression.
3. **Economy and Sponsorship Integration**: Add economic features and sponsorships.
4. **Match Simulation**: Code the match-play mechanics.
5. **Testing Phase**: Rigorously test each component for bugs and balance.
6. **Launch**: Release the game and gather player feedback.

### Conclusion
This comprehensive player career wheel game will provide an exciting and immersive experience for players, allowing them to navigate the ups and downs of a sports career while engaging in strategic decision-making and skill development.
